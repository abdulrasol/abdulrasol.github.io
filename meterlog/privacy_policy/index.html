Privacy Policy

Smart Multimeter Reader (Meter Log)

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Smart Multimeter Reader (the "App") is developed by Abdulrasol ("we", "us", or "our"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from our users. The App is designed to recognize and log multimeter readings using AI technology completely offline.

2. Locally Stored Data

The App stores your captured readings, dates, times, and units locally on your device's secure database. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Camera Access

The App requires camera access to capture images of your multimeter display for AI-powered OCR recognition. Images are processed locally and are not uploaded to the internet.

4. Storage Access

The App requires storage access to save exported Excel (.xlsx) and CSV files to your device's Documents folder at your request.

5. Third-Party Services

The App works completely offline and does not use third-party analytics or tracking services that collect personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Responsibilities

Users are responsible for the physical security of their device and the data stored within the App.
