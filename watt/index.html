The problem

Iraq has a power gap and a fragmented solar market

Demand is real, but discovery, comparison, and execution are still difficult.

Iraq faces chronic electricity shortages while having one of the strongest solar conditions in the region. Consumers and companies still struggle with an unstructured market, inconsistent pricing, and the lack of one trusted workflow from sizing to purchase and execution.

❌ 8-12 hours of daily power outages in many areas
❌ High generator and fuel costs
❌ Unstructured market for solar distributors and installers
❌ Difficult price and specification comparison across suppliers
