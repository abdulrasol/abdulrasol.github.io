The app may store session state, cached user info, and app settings (language, theme, preferences) on your device.

We use collected information to manage accounts, provide app features (calculations, marketplace, dashboard), process feedback, send notifications, and improve app performance.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers (backend, storage), Firebase (notifications), or for legal compliance and security reasons.

5. Permissions and Device Access

The app may request access to Camera (for images/barcodes), Photos (for uploads), Internet, and Notifications.

6. Password Reset and Account Deletion

Users can request password resets and account deletion within the app. Some records may be retained for legal or security reasons.
