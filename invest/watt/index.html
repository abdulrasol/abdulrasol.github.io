The first comprehensive solar energy platform for Iraq, ready to scale globally.

The Opportunity

Iraq's solar market is rapidly growing with increasing government and private sector investment in renewable energy. The residential and commercial sectors show high demand for reliable solar solutions. The platform can also serve global markets with its universal calculation features.

The Product

Solar system calculator and equipment marketplace for designing clean energy solutions. For homeowners, businesses, and solar professionals in Iraq and globally.

☀️

Solar System Calculator

Calculate system size, panel count, battery capacity, and inverter requirements for on-grid and off-grid setups.

🛒

Equipment Marketplace

Browse and compare solar panels, batteries, inverters, and accessories from multiple suppliers.

📊

Project Management

Track your solar installations, generate professional PDF quotes, and manage client proposals.

Market & Competition

Existing apps are either limited to basic calculators or focused only on equipment sales. Watt uniquely combines system design, marketplace, sales management, and advertising in one integrated platform for both end-users and companies.

Business Model

In-app purchases, subscription tiers for professionals and companies, advertising space for equipment suppliers, commission on marketplace transactions.
