When you use MEAAD, we may collect certain information from your Google account to provide our services, including:

  • Basic profile information via Google Login

  • Access to your Google Sheets to store and manage your data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our periodic payment and subscriber management services
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support

3. Data Storage and Protection (Google Drive)

MEAAD puts your data completely under your control. All subscriber and payment data is stored directly in your personal Google Drive account as a Google Sheet. The app requests access to Google Drive exclusively to create, read, and modify its own data file within your account to provide the cloud synchronization feature. The app developer has no access to your data, and it is never sent to any external servers. You are the sole owner of your data and we do not sell or share it with any third parties.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
