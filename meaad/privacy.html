1. Information We Collect

When you use MEAAD, we may collect certain information from your Google account to provide our services, including:

  • Basic profile information via Google Login

  • Access to your Google Sheets to store and manage your data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our periodic payment and subscriber management services

MEAAD stores your subscriber and payment data in Google Sheets that you create through the application. To ensure the application operates correctly, maintain service quality, and implement future improvements, these specific Google Sheets are shared with the MEAAD developer. We do not sell your personal information or your stored data to third parties.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
